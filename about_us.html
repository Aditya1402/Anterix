About Us

Club ANTERiX is a group of undergraduate students from VIT Bhopal University, founded in the year 2021. ANTERiX is VIT Bhopal’s first ever aviation club which will be dealing with the field of aviation and space technology. VIT Bhopal believes that this club has potential to drive the student’s passion for their field of interest and provide them the right knowledge. We help students from each and every branch to dive deep into the subject whether it may be coding, electronics, aerodynamics and any other mechanical work.

Our Mission

Humans are now in the Space era . Most nations are making development in avionics and space technologies. We the students of Club ANTERiX would focus on developments and innovation in the field of aero-designing, model rocketry and satellite designing, machine learning, and complex programming

Our Vision

Enabling students to gain knowledge and experience in the field of Satellite ,Space technologies and Aero-modelling as well as launching many fanatics from VIT Bhopal who can compete in many national and international level aero-designing and aero-modelling competitions .
